KB Clinic

DEC 31, 2023

KB Clinic Software, It's a program that helps the doctors and their clinics to handle their data correctly. the Program have 3 major modules (patient, doctor, admin).

the patient module (for the standard, advanced) is the module that shows to the patients all the doctors saved and their details and their categories.

the doctors module is the module that the doctors sign into it and see his prices, add his details, add patients, add records for every time a patients comes to him, add a prescription and print it, and much much more.

the admin module is when the admin sign in, the admin is the person that see all of the transactions, have the ability to delete a doctor, delete patient, add a new user (doctor or another admin), edit the prices of every doctor after the doctor adding them. KB Clinic
